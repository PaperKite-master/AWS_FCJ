### General Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**
The working environment is welcoming and easy to adapt to. Everyone in the FCJ team is supportive and approachable, making it easier for me to stay focused and feel comfortable. I truly appreciate the balance between professionalism and friendliness.

**2. Support from Mentor / Admin Team**
The mentor and admin team consistently provided guidance, explained concepts clearly, and were always ready to help when I encountered difficulties. Their timely direction helped me progress faster and feel confident in handling tasks.

**3. Relevance Between Tasks and Academic Background**
The tasks assigned aligned well with what I have learned at school and also introduced new practical knowledge. This combination kept me motivated and broadened my skill set effectively.

**4. Learning Opportunities & Skill Development**
I had the chance to join useful workshops that offered practical knowledge and new perspectives. Working with the team also improved my communication, teamwork, and professional working skills.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**
The FCJ team demonstrates a strong sense of teamwork. Everyone is willing to share experiences and support each other. This positive culture made me feel connected and valued, even as an intern.

**6. Policies & Benefits for Interns**
The company provides clear policies and offers flexible working hours, helping me balance school and internship responsibilities. Having access to internal training sessions is also a major advantage.
